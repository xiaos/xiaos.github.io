This Privacy Policy describes how do we collects, uses and share personal information about users of our mobile applications.


Personal Information We Collect:


We don't collect any personal information when you download and use our apps, we don't even have a server to store your personal information. The only way we get your personal information is when you contact/feedback to us by email, we will know your email address, we guarantee we will only use your email to communicate with you. We will never use your information(email) for any other purpose.



Mobile App Permissions Requested:


In order to provide you the service and improve the app quality, we request the below permissions on your device


  • WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE: Save photos created by this app into your device.
  • CAMERA: Allow the app to use your camera to take photos or capture video.
  • RECORD_AUDIO: Allow the app to capture video with audio.
  • INTERNET and ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: Access internet to request ads and report crash/bugs.
